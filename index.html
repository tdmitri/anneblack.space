Mies, my great mentor said: “Test test test.” I study Games Design and Development at the University of Greenwich. This website is a portfolio of my visual design works.

For us Graphic Design is “organization of information.” There are other types of graphic design more concerned with illustration or of a narrative nature. Nothing could be more useful to reach our intention than the Grid. The grid represents the basic structure of our graphic design, it helps to organize the content, it provides consistency.
